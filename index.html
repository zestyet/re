Never Have I Ever

Never Have I Ever is a coming-of-age comedy-drama television series created by Mindy Kaling and Lang Fisher. The series follows the life of Devi Vishwakumar, a first-generation Indian American teenager living in California.

Devi, played by Maitreyi Ramakrishnan, navigates the challenges of high school while dealing with the recent loss of her father. With her friends Eleanor and Fabiola, Devi experiences the ups and downs of teenage life, including romance, friendship, and cultural identity.

The series explores themes of identity, family dynamics, and the complexities of teenage emotions. It has received critical acclaim for its authentic portrayal of adolescence and its diverse cast.
